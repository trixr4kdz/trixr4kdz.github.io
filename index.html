About Me

I am a recent college graduate from Loyola Marymount University, Los Angeles, CA. I received my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science with a minor in Applied Mathematics on May 6, 2017. Over my time at university, I participated in research projects and REUs (Research Experience for Undergraduates) that contributed to my growth as a software developer. One of them is called GRNmap, which you can check out by going to the GRNmap project site or by viewing the source code on my GitHub repo. Right now, I'm currently working with the Radii Robotics startup to create the first ever telepresence drone that provides 2-way video calling and remote control using a web browser.
